FOUNDER

Mr Wong Chung Wei

Our founder Mr Wong Chung Wei

Grew up with a dream inspired by the legendary Malaysian badminton player, Dato Lee Chong Wei. From a young age, he admired Chong Wei's determination, skill, and sportsmanship, and he aspired to follow in his hero's footsteps.

With his high school teacher's mentorship, he soared to international heights. Stepping onto the international stage, Ali carried not just his dream but prove that inspiration, coupled with resilience, could turn a humble dream into a remarkable reality.

Grateful, Mr Wong start a badminton club, becoming the mentor he once sought, ensuring dreams thrived by the youngsters nowadays that have a same dreams as he was.

INSPIRATION

Dato Lee Chong Wei

Dato Lee Chong Wei, a retired Malaysian badminton player

Widely regarded as one of the greatest singles players in the history of the sport. Born on October 21, 1982, in Bagan Serai, Perak, Malaysia, Chong Wei's remarkable career spanned from the early 2000s to 2019.

His playing style was characterized by his speed, agility, and powerful strokes. He achieved numerous accolades throughout his career, including multiple Olympic and World Championship silver medals. He became the world No. 1-ranked men's singles player several times, showcasing his dominance in the badminton world.

Beyond his achievements on the court, He is celebrated for his sportsmanship, humility, and contributions to Malaysian sports. In recognition of his outstanding career, he was conferred with the title "Dato" by the Malaysian government, a prestigious honor.

Dato Lee Chong Wei's impact extends beyond his playing days, inspiring aspiring badminton players worldwide and leaving an indelible mark on the sport's history. His story is one of talent, perseverance, and a commitment to excellence in the world of badminton.
