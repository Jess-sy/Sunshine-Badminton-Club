Membership

Basic
Membership

  • Accessible to badminton courts only if courts are available.
  • No coaching sessions.
  • Members may charge drop-in fees if invite guests to play.
  • Able to join other member's friendly games.

Price: RM 60/month

Standard
Membership

  • Accessible to badminton courts.
  • Regular coaching sessions for skill improvement.
  • Participation in club tournaments and competitions.
  • Discounts on club merchandise.

Price: RM 120/month

Sign Up

Premium
Membership

  • Unlimited access to badminton courts at all times.
  • Priority access on booking badminton courts.
  • Regular coaching sessions.
  • Personalized one-on-one coaching sessions.
  • Priority registration for tournaments and events.
  • Specialized training camps and workshops.
  • Discounts on merchandise and club events.

Price: RM 180/month

Sign Up

More details and Information:

  • Yearly membership renewal is available.
  • Various payment options (credit/debit card, online transfer, etc.) are accepted for membership fees.
  • Online registration through the website with an easy-to-use signup form.
  • Access to training resources, newsletters, and community events.

For Non Members:

  • Courts can be book by call, walk in or email.
  • Non-members only can use court if the court is empty or cancel by member.
  • Price are charged per hour
  • Shuttlecocks is not provided

Price: RM 18/hour
